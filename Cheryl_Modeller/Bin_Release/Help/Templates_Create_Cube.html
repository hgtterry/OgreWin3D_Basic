Templates Create Cube
 


Intro: The Cube is one of the fundamental geometric shapes that can be used to construct and shape the world you envision.
- You will be able to manually put whatever measurements you precisely want your object to have from wall sizes, thickness and width, etc..


Style:- There is variety of different styles that you could choose from starts by
-Solid: The term solid refers to a fully filled, three-dimensional object with no internal voids or hollow spaces. It indicates that the interior is completely occupied by material, leaving no room to insert or place anything within it.
-Hollow: A hollow geometric shape is defined by an empty interior space, which can either remain open or be structured with additional forms within it.
 Cut Brush: Cut Brush refers to a geometric shape capable of cutting through any other form, fully replacing the intersected space with its own volume.
Position: Gives you the option to choose whether you want to install your geometric shape right at the centre of the world or right at the spot where the camera is.
