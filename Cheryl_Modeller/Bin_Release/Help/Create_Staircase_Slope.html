Create Staircase and Slope
 


ntro: The Staircase/Slope  is one of the fundamental geometric shapes that can be used to construct and shape the world you envision.
- You will be able to manually put whatever measurements you precisely want your object to have from height, width and number of steps, etc.
- The Update button instantly reflects the measurement changes applied to the geometric shape, allowing you to preview adjustments before finalizing its insertion into the 3D environment


Style:- There is variety of different styles that you could choose from starts by
Stairs: A style that allows a slope with -X- amount of stairs.
Ramp: A style that allows you create a flat angled ramp.
 Cut Brush: Cut Brush refers to a geometric shape capable of cutting through any other form, fully replacing the intersected space with its own volume

Position:- Gives you the option to choose whether you want to install your geometric shape right at the centre of the world or right at the spot where the camera is.
