Properties Templates
 

-The Templates menu provides a complete selection of geometric shapes that you can use as building blocks for constructing your own 3D world.

- Each of these buttons listed below would open a whole new page that gives you the ability to manually put whatever measurements you precisely want your object to have from wall sizes, thickness and width, etc.

Intro: each one of those shapes ARE one of the fundamental geometric shapes that can be used to construct and shape the world you envision.
- The Update button instantly reflects the measurement changes applied to the geometric shape, allowing you to preview adjustments before finalizing its insertion into the 3D environment
- The Round button allows you to adjust the corners, enabling you to define whether the angles remain sharp or transition into smooth, circular curves.
- The Rectangular button allows you to create a sharp corners arch which could be simplified as rectangulars connected togethers.

Style:- There are 4 different styles that you could choose from starts by
-Solid: The term solid refers to a fully filled, three-dimensional object with no internal voids or hollow spaces. It indicates that the interior is completely occupied by material, leaving no room to insert or place anything within it.
-Hollow: A hollow geometric shape is defined by an empty interior space, which can either remain open or be structured with additional forms within it.
Ring:
Stairs: A style that allows a slope with -X- amount of stairs.
Ramp: A style that allows you create a flat angled ramp.
Cut Brush: Cut Brush refers to a geometric shape capable of cutting through any other form, fully replacing the intersected space with its own volume.
